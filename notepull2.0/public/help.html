Get started with notePull


To create a new note

1. Click on an existing folder (there is a default created for you already).
2. Click "Create new note"

To highlight a bullet

1. Click on the middle icon on the bottom toolbar to select a color.
2. Select the bullet you want highlighted.
3. Click on the left icon on the bottom toolbar to highlight.

To change your password

1. Click on "Settings" on the left menu.
2. Type your new password twice and save.
